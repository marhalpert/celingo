Interpreting

Conference Interpreting

Simultaneous/conference interpreting is used in large-scale conferences.The interpreters sit in a booth and speak into a microphone. They listen to the speaker through earphones and immediately translate into the other language. The audience uses portable receivers to hear the interpreters in the corresponding language.

Consecutive Interpreting

Consecutive interpreting is generally used in meetings with a small number of participants or delegates. This method is more time consuming, but it works well in community settings or meetings that aim to be more interactive over a shorter period of time.
